Enrollment for Grade XI

This application takes the grades and the GPA of the students who just have graduated from Grade 10 and by the rules and standards set by the Ministry of Education of Nepal, this app shows the courses available for the student to enroll in Grade XI as per the grades and GPA scored. Here, I have made available the 64-bit installer for Windows to install on your computer.

This application totally relies over the JavaScript and doesn't require contact from any application server. Therefore, I have made it as effective as possible to run on the client-side.

Application will be available on other platforms (macOS and linux) soon.
